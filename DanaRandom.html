Life Pro Tips

How to improve your life (trust me, as I teenager I know everything)

Ice Cream

Eat ice cream with a smaller spoon, that way it lasts longer. Also, never eat from the container, because you'll never realise how much is gone.

Laundry

Change your laundry day to Friday. As time goes on and you feel like it's been so long since the weekend, remember the last time you did laundry, and it will suddenly feel much closer.

Ideas

Write down ideas the second you get them, that way you never forget and if you're bored you have a go-to.

Trash

Whenever you go hiking, bring a plastic bag with you and pick up every piece of trash you see on the way. Once you get to the top, discard the waste in the trashcan so it's not heavy, and carry on your way down.

Productivity

If a task will take you less than five minutes, do it the second it crosses your mind.

Netflix

S on your keyboard can act as the skip intro function.
