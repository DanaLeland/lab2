Dingle

/dean-gull/

When someobody is doing something stupid, but not to the level where "idiot" is justified.

Dangle

/dan-gull/

When a dingle is becomming increasingly stupid, and on the verge of being called an idiot. Alternative use; "idiot" when children are around.

Of a Flip Flap

/uv, ay, flip, flap/

An extension, usually tied to the end of a word, to show discontent.

"A Hidden Gem"

/ā, hidn, jem/

To define a song one has heard a million times, where everybody finds it agreeable, however remains totally *underrated.* (e.g Bohemian Rhapsody.) Originated in the Dylan Dynasty.
